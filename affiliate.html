FAQ

How much does it cost to join?

Joining the affiliate program is free.

Who can apply?

When do I get paid?

Monthly bank transfer upon invoice.

How is the tracking of my referrals handled?

Tracking is fully automated. You have two options to link a client to your account:

Affiliate link: This is the simplest method. The client clicks your personalized link and a cookie stores your identifier. If they subscribe, they are automatically attributed to you.

Affiliate code: You can also share a specific code that the client enters during sign-up.

Where can I find these? Your unique link and tracking code are available at any time in your partner dashboard inside your Zyllio account.
