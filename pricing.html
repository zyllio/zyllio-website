Frequently asked questions

Does Zyllio offer a free plan ?


Yes. we offer a completely free plan that you can use for as long as you want

Does Zyllio need my credit card to sign up for the free plan ?


No, you don't need to enter your credit card information to sign up the free plan

Is there a minimum commitment period for the subscription?


No, our subscription is commitment-free. You can cancel your subscription at any time, with no extra fees or restrictions.

Can I upgrade to a paid plan later on ?


Yes, you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time if you need more advanced features or want to remove any limitations that come with the free plan

Does Zyllio offer unlimited apps ?


Yes, our product offers unlimited apps with a fair usage policy. The fair usage policy ensures that Zyllio is used reasonably and not abused. This means that we reserve the right to take action if we detect any activity that is deemed excessive, unusual or unauthorised

What happens on usage limits ?


When you reach your plan limit, Zyllio limits the amount of data displayed on mobile devices. This means that you will only be able to view a subset of your data, based on the plan you are currently subscribed to

Can I publish my Zyllio application on the App Store and Google Play?


No, Zyllio applications are accessible via an internet link (QR Code). Not publishing on the app stores keeps you independent and simplifies the distribution process, making it instant.
